About Me

I grew up with feet planted firmly on both sides of the digital divide. Armed with a Packard Bell 486DX2 and my family's wood and machine shop, 9 year old me started down my lifelong path of lets-try-this-and-see-what-happens. This approach has led me to acquire a very mixed and interesting skill-set. In Summer 2016 I relocated to Rochester with my wife and kids.

What I Do

Web Development & IT

I have experience in both development and desktop repair/networking. Through the years I've tinkered with platforms from Geocities to AWS. I've built and upgraded computers and pulled ethernet through ceilings and walls. I am currently enrolled at WGU in the Bachelor’s of Science in Cloud Computing Program.

Optics

I started at Optimax in 2018 with no prior experience in optics and have since learned a great deal about precision optical manufacturing and metrology. It is quite an experience to work on parts where a micron can be a relatively huge tolerance.

Fabrication

I have extensive experience making things of all types. I am comfortable on manual lathes and Bridgeport mills, as well as MIG, TIG, and oxyacetylene welding. I've made fine hardwood furniture and have done my share of residential remodeling. I have electronics experience with Arduino and electronic circuitry as well as residential wiring.

Visuals

I enjoy photography and I am skilled at image manipulation with Adobe Photoshop.I have experience designing menus, brochures, posters and logos.
